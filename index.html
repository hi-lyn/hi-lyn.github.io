ABOUT ME

Meet Lyn,
The Story So Far —

I'm a passionate UI/UX product designer dedicated to crafting stunning and user-friendly online experiences.

As a Product Designer with proficiency in UI & UX, I take pride in crafting strong and graceful solutions. I have more than 4 years included UI/UX design and Graphic design of experience in tech. I'm presently employed at Beincom.

I specialize in creating websites that not only look great but also provide a top-notch user experience. My journey in web design began with a fascination for the ever-evolving world of technology and design. Over the years, I've honed my skills to transform complex concepts into visually appealing, user-friendly websites.

Three friends with a shared passion for AI met at Stanford's AI Lab. After countless coffee-fueled coding sessions, we got into YC's Winter '20 batch. Today, backed by Silicon Valley's finest, we're making AI accessible to businesses worldwide.

FAQ

Burning Questions About Lyn

Do you only design web apps?

I specialize in responsive web apps and dashboards, but I’ve also designed native mobile UIs and systems for SaaS tools.

Both! I’ve worked in cross-functional teams, and I also lead designs independently on client projects.

Can you build the site too?

I don’t develop production code, but I deliver dev-ready files, prototyping links, and detailed design specs for engineers.
